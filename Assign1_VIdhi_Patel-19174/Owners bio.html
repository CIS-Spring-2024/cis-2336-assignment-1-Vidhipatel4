The UH Bistro

About Vidhi

Early Years

Vidhi was born and raised in Houston, Texas. She is currently a student at the University of Houston and loves serving in the culinary department of the Sugar Land campus of University of Houston. Vidhi has been on her culinary journey for about 3 years, and throughout that journey, she has developed some culinary skills like service, presentation on the plate, the taste of her food, and building her techniques.

Opening of The UH Bistro

Vidhi has always had a passion for food, and she has always wanted to start her own business. Even though she is young and still in college, she opened a Bistro at the University of Houston - Sugar Land. With the help of her family and friends, her culinary pursuits became a reality. While being a college student, she has been able to focus on school and her bistro since they are all in the same place.

About Vidhi outside of the Bistro

When Vidhi isn't spending her time at the bistro, she enjoys spending time with her friends and family. She loves to explore new stuff like coffee, outdoor activities, and shopping. Even though she loves food, she has a side of her where she loves to bake.

Join Vidhi Patel on her culinary odyssey, where every bite tells a story and every meal is an invitation to savor the moment.
